Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our jersey design and printing services

How long does it take to complete an order?

Standard orders take 5-7 business days, while bulk orders (25+ jerseys) can be completed in 3-5 days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.

What file formats do you accept for designs?

We accept all major design formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, and JPG. Vector formats (AI, EPS) are preferred for the best quality results.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our professional design team can create custom logos, team branding, and complete jersey designs from scratch or work with your existing concepts.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We have no minimum order quantity! Whether you need 1 jersey or 1000, we're happy to help with orders of any size.
